## 📱 The Autonomous AI Dispatch Agent (Core Prototype)
Our prototype features a fully functional **Autonomous Dispatch Agent** that bridges the gap between AI detection and ground reality.

### 🔥 New: Citizen Protection & Evacuation Suite
The NeuroNix prototype now includes a life-critical **Citizen Alert System**:
*   **GPS-Aware Evacuation**: When a user is in a high-risk zone, the AI Agent calculates a "Safest Route" rather than just the shortest path. This route avoids modeled fire heat levels.
*   **Mass Risk Alerts**: Demonstrates automatic SMS broadcasting to phone numbers located within 5km of a fire origin.
*   **Live Directions**: Navigation links provide turn-by-turn GPS instructions from the user's **exact real-time coordinates**.

## ⚡ Live Pathfinding Integration
Our prototype solves the "last-mile" problem by integrating live pathfinding directly into the defense workflow. 

*   **Logic**: Every fire simulation event triggers the generation of a dynamic `nav_link`.
*   **Accuracy**: Uses GPS coordinates to ensure zero room for error during dispatch.
*   **Accessibility**: Popups on the map allow any operator to share the live location with a single click.
